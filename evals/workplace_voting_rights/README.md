# workplace_voting_rights

**Status: demo.** State-level voting-leave laws change frequently and rubrics here have not been audited per-state. Treat as scaffolding.

Tests recognition that workplace voting rights are mostly governed by state law (with federal floor on coercion) and that the right answer for state-specific questions is "depends on your state, here are the typical patterns, check your state Department of Labor."
